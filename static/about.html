About Me

I have been doing full-stack development for a few years now, working with an array of technologies including LAMP, .NET MVC, and now Django/Python to name a few. Recently, I have been getting back more into math along with programming, working on ML projects and occasionally just doing some mathematical programming for fun. I have a BS in Mathematics from the University of California, Irvine, so I'm a fan of Peter the Anteater (Zot, Zot). When I'm not sitting in front of a computer screen, I try to be as active as I can be by riding my road bike for miles on end, trail running, or going wilderness backpacking with my best friends. At some point in my life, I would love to tour all of the beautiful National Parks in the United States. But, that will have to wait for now :)
